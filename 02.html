Decision making on the chosen object - The Box. Our considering factors were based on the potential of success, possibilities of actions and objective of the module. It was a tough choice between the Swivel Chair and the Box because both were very promising. In the end, the box won because of the greater possibility of actions it could perform compared to the chair.

Evolution of the chosen form.


An 80mm cube, self-made using self-cut acrylic or plywood and connected with finger joints. The lid is not obvious but is removable.


A regular and larger rectangular packaging box.


A smaller and shallower rectangular box with a lid. But this aspect ratio and box depth hinted at some specific objects that could fit within, so we decided to work on a cube instead.


A cube with a removable lid. But if we wanted our users to instinctively throw the box, this method of opening may create a vulnerable spot on the box as the lid has a tendency to fall out.


A cube with a lid. This form was a choice with best of both worlds.
