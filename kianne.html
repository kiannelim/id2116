Kianne is a Singaporean industrial designer. Her story started with a delicious cup of fine arabica at a local coffee roastery where she worked alongside state-of-the-art coffee equipment. Captivated by how design and technology can come together to craft a lovely beverage and bringing smiles to the table, she endeavours to design meaningful experiences. Her design strives for a balance between elegance and practicality.
